Ave Mujica

Biography

Ave Mujica is a Japanese girl band from the BanG Dream! Girls Band Party which formed in 2023.The group has five members which is Misumi Uika (Doloris) on lead guitar and vocals,Wakaba Mutsumi (Mortis) on rhythm guitar,Yahata Umiri (Timoris) on bass,Yuutenji Nyamu (Amoris) on drums,and Togawa Sakiko (Oblivionis) on keyboard.
The band is part of BanG Dream!'s 2.5-dimensional project, where the voice actresses not only provide the voices and musical performances for their anime characters but also perform live concerts in Japan, bringing the fictional band to life on stage.Their music and visual style are heavily influenced by gothic and theatrical elements, creating a dark and elegant atmosphere that sets them apart from other bands in the franchise.
